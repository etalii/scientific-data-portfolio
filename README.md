# Scientific data portfolio
### Applying scientific thinking to data analytics

## Hi, I'm Andrés 👋

I'm a biologist graduated from the **University of Buenos Aires (UBA)** with a background in scientific research, university teaching and quantitative data analysis.

For more than a decade I have worked with complex biological datasets, statistical modelling and reproducible research. My work has required transforming raw data into reliable evidence to answer scientific questions about disease ecology and environmental systems.

Today I am expanding that experience towards **Data Analytics** and **AI Evaluation**, applying the same scientific mindset to solve analytical problems beyond academia.

---

## 🔬 From scientific research to data analytics

Scientific research is fundamentally a data-driven discipline.

Every research project requires:

- defining meaningful questions
- collecting and integrating data
- cleaning noisy datasets
- applying statistical methods
- validating assumptions
- communicating evidence clearly

These are the same core skills expected from a Data Analyst.

Rather than changing careers completely, I am extending years of experience in quantitative scientific research into new domains where rigorous data analysis creates value.
